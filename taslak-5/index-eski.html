About MrML Lab

Nestled at the intersection of machines, mechanics, and biological intelligence, MrML Lab is dedicated to unraveling the fundamental codes of the brain and translating them into next-generation mechatronic and software systems.

From high-dimensional neural data analysis to the deployment of foundation models for medicine, our mission is to build the intelligent interfaces that will bridge the gap between artificial cognition and biological reality.

Research Areas

Core pillars of our scientific inquiry

Computational Biology

Simulating complex biological networks, protein folding, and cellular dynamics using deep geometric learning.

Neural Data Analysis

Decoding high-dimensional neural signals to understand cognition, perception, and memory encoding.

Bioinformatics

Developing scalable pipelines for genomic sequence analysis and multimodal biomedical data integration.

AI for Medicine

Predictive modeling for personalized diagnostics, treatment optimization, and mechatronic prosthesis control.

Lab Equipment

State-of-the-art facilities powering our research

High-Resolution Neural Imaging Setup

Captures in-vivo neural signals with microsecond precision for real-time decoding.

HPC GPU Cluster

Dedicated compute nodes for training massive biological foundation and transformer models.

Mechatronic Prosthesis Testbed

Advanced robotic limbs interfaced directly with closed-loop neural decoders.
