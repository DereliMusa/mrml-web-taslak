About MrML Lab

MrML Lab is a multidisciplinary research group focused on developing machine learning methods for understanding complex biological systems. Our work bridges artificial intelligence, neuroscience, and computational biology.

Research Areas

Computational Biology

Developing machine learning models to analyze biological networks and cellular systems.
